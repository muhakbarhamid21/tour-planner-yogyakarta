About Us

Welcome to Tour Planner Yogyakarta

Tour Planner Yogyakarta is your ultimate travel companion for exploring the cultural and natural wonders of Yogyakarta. We understand that with the abundance of destinations, choosing the perfect one can be overwhelming. That's why we offer a personalized decision support system to recommend destinations based on your preferences, ensuring a seamless and memorable travel experience.

With Tour Planner Yogyakarta, you can discover the hidden gems of this vibrant city, from its historic temples to its pristine beaches, adventurous treks, and rich educational sites. Whether you are planning a cultural journey, a nature retreat, or an educational tour, we have you covered.

Our platform is designed to help both tourists and local businesses manage and promote destinations, contributing to the sustainable development of Yogyakarta’s tourism industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make your Yogyakarta experience exceptional by offering tailored recommendations, simplifying your travel planning, and ensuring that you visit the destinations that best match your interests.
